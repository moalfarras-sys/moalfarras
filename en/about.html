Short Story

My name is Mohammad Alfarras, born on 05.09.1994 in Hasakah, Syria. I grew up in a simple environment with school, university and early family responsibilities, before my life took a completely new direction when I moved to Germany in 2015.

After arriving in Germany I went through a long phase of waiting, paperwork and adaptation, then started language courses from A1 up to C1, moving between different schools, integration courses and real-life experiences in a new country and culture.

Later I decided to focus on two main pillars: building a stable career in logistics and customer service, and developing my skills in accounting and business software such as Lexware, DATEV and Microsoft Dynamics NAV ‚Äì in addition to solid Office skills (Excel, Word, PowerPoint).

I worked in several jobs in Germany, from manual production and warehouse-related work, to an internship at Siemens, to different roles in small and medium companies, until I reached my current position at Rhenus Home Delivery in Berlin as a dispatching and logistics employee.

I like to share a realistic picture of life and work in Europe, so I started a YouTube channel and online presence where I talk about my journey, mistakes, and lessons learned ‚Äì without exaggeration or fake success stories.
